Project Overview

In the interests of assisting and accelerating the processes of using bacteria to synthesize the proteins found in meat, researchers have been cataloguing data pertaining to the bacterial species found within the belly buttons of individuals participating in a clinical trial. The purpose of this project was to create a “web page dashboard” wherein the “ID” of a specific individual could be selected and a bar graph displaying the 10 most common bacteria found in their belly button, a gauge indicating their frequency of belly button scrubbing and a bubble chart displaying the relative amounts of all bacterial species found in their belly button would be displayed.
